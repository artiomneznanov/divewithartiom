About Me

With over 10 years of freediving experience and international certification, I bring passion and expertise to every lesson. My teaching philosophy centers on safety, mindfulness, and progressive skill development.

I've trained with world champions and have competed internationally, but my true passion is helping others discover the transformative power of freediving.
